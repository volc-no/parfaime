ABOUT

Parfaime was created as an online outlet to capture the essence of luxury and chicness. The name combines the two words “parfait” and “perfume” to represent the sweet personality behind all perfumes whether it be gifted or loved by the buyer. Our team is passionate about delivering fragrances that meet every customer's needs. We take pride in the sincerity of our dedication to presenting extensively researched collections to express our individuality and commitment to the brand.

PURPOSE

We exist to deliver sensational experiences through the craftsmanship of scents and evoke feelings of confidence for our users. We strongly believe that perfume is a form of artistry and expression! Every perfume is different and can enhance any experience. We aim to celebrate moments with our customers through our collections.
